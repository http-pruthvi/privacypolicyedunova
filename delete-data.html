Data Deletion Request

EDUNOVA - Educational App

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

⚠️ Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data including notes, progress, and rewards will be permanently deleted.

Methods to Delete Your Data

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the EDUNOVA app
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Tap on Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision
  6. Your account will be deleted within 30 days

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to: support@devxaitechnologies.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request - EDUNOVA

Include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your request within 7 business days.

Send Email Request

Method 3: Contact Form

Use the in-app feedback form:

  1. Open EDUNOVA app
  2. Go to Profile → Help & Support
  3. Select Send Feedback
  4. Choose category: Account Deletion
  5. Submit your request

What Gets Deleted?

  • Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Academic information (institution, branch, year)
  • Uploaded notes and study materials
  • Downloaded content and cache
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Rewards and coins balance
  • Activity history and progress

What We Retain?

For legal and security purposes, we may retain:

  • Transaction records (as required by law)
  • Anonymized analytics data
  • Data required for ongoing legal proceedings

This data cannot be linked back to you and is retained only as required by law.

Timeline

  • In-App Deletion: Immediate (processed within 30 days)
  • Email Request: 7 business days to process
  • Complete Deletion: Within 30 days of request
ℹ️ Note: After deletion, you can create a new account anytime, but your previous data cannot be recovered.
